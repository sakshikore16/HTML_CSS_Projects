Sahara

The Sahara Desert is Earth's largest hot desert, stretching across North Africa, characterized by vast stretches of sand dunes, rocky plateaus, and scarce vegetation. With extreme temperatures and minimal rainfall, it's one of the world's most challenging environments.

Western Desert

The Western Desert is a vast arid region in Egypt, characterized by sandy plains, rocky plateaus, and sparse vegetation.
